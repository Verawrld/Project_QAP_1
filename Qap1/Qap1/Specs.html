Under The Hood

The Ford Focus RS has a 2.3L EcoBoost engine that produces 350 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. The car has a top speed of 165 mph and can go from 0-60 in 4.6 seconds. The car has a six-speed manual transmission and all-wheel drive. The car also has a drift mode that allows the driver to drift the car around corners.
