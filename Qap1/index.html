Introduction

The Ford Focus rs entered the last year in the market in 2018. the Ford's hardcore hatch back had a 350 horse powered engine turbocharged, with a four-cylinder engine and was a six speed manual transmission. The car was a four wheel drive and had a drift mode. The car was a limited edition and was not puchased by many people, and thats the reason they stopped the production of the car. The car was a very good car and was very fast and had a very good handling.
